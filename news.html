Wave 2 DLC is out now! Expand your heroic quest in Hyrule Warriors: Age of Calamity.

In the Hyrule Warriors: Age of Calamity game, you can see Hyrule 100 years before the events of the Legend of Zelda™: Breath of the Wild game (in a very action-y, “take on hundreds of enemies with really sharp objects” kind of way).
